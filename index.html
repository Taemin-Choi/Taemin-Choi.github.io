About Me

I am a robotics software engineer.

Since 2020, I have been working as an intern researcher at the KIST(Korea Institute of Science and Technology). I completed a master's degree in Mechanical Engineering at Korea University in Academy-Research-Industry Cooperation Program while working in the KIST. Before my MSc I did undergraduate in Mechanical Engineering at the Seoul National University of Science and Technology.

I have been developing an algorithm to enhance performance of the visual recognition system for mobile robot. In simple terms, I want to make robots that can learn like children who learn by questioning their parents about what they don't know continuously.
